Safe Steps

A chutes & ladders game about catching risky behavior. Spin, move, and when you reach a ladder or a chute, answer a safety question to climb — or to keep from sliding back. Questions are drawn at random, so each landing is a fresh one.

Spin to begin
Players:
Player 1
Climb to space 50
Ladder — 1 right of 4 to climb Chute — both right of 2 to stay
How to play: On your turn, press Spin (1–10) and your token moves that many spaces — you can land anywhere. Land on the base of a ladder and answer one question correctly to climb up. Land on the top of a chute and you must pick both correct actions to keep your footing; miss and you slide down. Reach space 50 to win — first one there takes it.
